Fire Scores

Sculptures

Build a sculpture with metal and wood.

Send the sculpture, lighter fluid, and a pack of matches to a friend.

Attach the following message:

"Douse this sculpture in lighter fluid and watch it burn completely."

Succulents

Collect small leaves of succulents.

Sit in a quiet room with the lights off.

Hold a lighter so that the tip of the flame is close to a leaf.

Slowly move the lighter upward until the leaf is inside the flame.

Continue burning.
