Archival Music [2020]

Description

In my practice I create various tuning systems to work with. At the time of this composition I had created three systems. The following two pieces are an effort to archive the differences in pitch content. The three tuning systems are assigned to a string of a unison from A3-G#4. The first piece arranges the pitches of the three systems from low to high and uses sine tones to compare the difference in pitch to equal temperament. The second piece lists all adjacent unisons from wide to narrow, which in this case ends in a perfect unison.
