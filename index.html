Jesse Reyes

Jesse Reyes is an LA based composer, pianist, and technician. The intersection of these three specialties informs a practice of research and experimentation regarding the piano as a historical art object. The results of this practice are listed here as studies. He received his BFA from CalArts in the Performer-Composer program, studying composition with David Rosenboom, piano with Vicki Ray, and piano technology with Alan Eder. Jesse is one half of limitduo.
