Jesse Reyes

Jesse Reyes is an LA based composer, pianist, and piano technician. He received his BFA from CalArts in the Performer-Composer program, studying composition with David Rosenboom, piano with Vicki Ray, and piano technology with Alan Eder. Studies are listed here. Other works are posted on bandcamp. A web-based installation can be found here.
