About Retuned Piano

installation music for piano retuned to rational/irrational intonation with probability-based combinations via max/msp.
Max/msp patch by jack dettling.

Four 12-tone pitch sets, each separated by 25c, are tuned so that the subdominant and dominant are untempered and the mediant, submediant and leading tone are lowered by a syntonic comma. The remaining pitches are set to OTS P equal temperament.
12 sets of predetermined randomly generated 12-tone rows are played by max/msp.

_

Generated melodic/harmonic material is to be considered as such;
an effort to remove a compositional action
within a conceptual formal structure.
