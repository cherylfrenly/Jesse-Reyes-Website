Digital Installation Room

Installation music for piano:
in rational/irrational intonation with probability-based combinations via max/msp.
Max/msp patch by jack dettling.

_

Four sets of 12tone pitch sets separated by 25c with the subdominant and dominant untempered and the mediant, submediant and leading tone lowered by a syntonic comma.
12 sets of predetermined and randomly generated 12-tone rows are played by max/msp.

_

This webpage is a digital substitute for an installation. In opening this page, you can begin to conceive of the current space you are in as the physical installation space.
The process of the max patch has no finite duration, but an excerpt is posted here. The length of the recording should accommodate a number of situations:
walks, drives, meditation, errands, tasks, etc.
