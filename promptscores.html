Prompt Scores [2019]

Description of Event

The excerpts which make up this album are from my mid-residence and graduation shows which took place at The Wild Beast (version 1) and the Roy O. Disney concert hall (version 2), respectively.

Version 1 (ensemble)

Someone will select a graphic score from a pile. They will set it on top, and whoever wants to participate will join them.

The person who selected the score will use numerical fingers to assign an order for the following process.

1st player

Look at the image.

Play an idea based on the creative impulse generated by the image.

This idea should be conceived of as a suggestion and not developed by the performer.

Stop and listen to the next player.

...

2nd player

Do not overlap with the previous player.

Play your own ‚Äúsuggestion‚Äù based on the creative impulse generated by the image.

Stop and listen to the next player.

...

Continue until all players have played their idea; collectively pause for a short moment.

Using the sounded motives as a starting point, improvise as much or as little as you want.

Version 2 (solo)

At your own disgretion, relate your ideas to the graphic prompts.

As you seek new ideas, find inspiration in the graphic prompts.
