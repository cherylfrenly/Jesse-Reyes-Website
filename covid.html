In light of the COVID-19 pandemic, I have outlined some procedures to help ensure our safety. The information presented here is extracted from the CDC guidelines which are linked here.

  • Please wear a mask while we are near each other.
  • If possible, please open doors or windows to help ventilate the room.
  • Try to maintain 6 feet of distance at all times.
  • If you or anyone in your household is experiencing symptoms, please reschedule your appointment.
  • If this results in a last-minute cancellation there is no associated fee.
  • If you or a family member test positive before or immediately after the appointment, please notify me.
  • Please remove any objects from on top of the piano so that I am touching as few things in your household as possible.

Thank you, your consideration is greatly appreciated!
